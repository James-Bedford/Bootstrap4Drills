Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker is a pivotal figure in the Rebel Alliance's struggle against the Glatic Empire, a friend and eventual brother -in law of smuggler Han Solo, and unknown to him until Return of the Jedi, the twin brother of Rebellion leader Princess Leia.

Han Solo

Han Solo is a smuggler and a former member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, a group of rebels who have been secretly recruited by the Empire to aid in the destruction of the Galactic Empire.

Princess Leia

Princess Leia is a former member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, a group of rebels who have been secretly recruited by the Empire to aid in the destruction of the Galactic Empire.

Chewbacca

He is a Wookiee, a member of the Rebel Alliance, and a former member of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, a group of rebels who have been secretly recruited by the Empire to aid in the destruction of the Galactic Empire.

Obi Wan Kenobi

In the original trilogy, he is a mentor to Luke Skywalker, and a former pupil of the Jedi Master Yoda.

Mara Jade

She appears in the now non-canon Legends series, as the wife of Luke Skywalker and mother of Ben Skywalker.
